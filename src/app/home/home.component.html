WELCOME TO GACC...

Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Govt Arts & Commerce College Indore(National Premier Institute) had been integral part of nationally reputed Holkar College Indore. Holkar College was established in year 1891 and Science, Arts, Commerce law Faculties were conducted combined in it. Holkar College was well respected & popular at state national level. Due to this, number of students increased every year & necessity of new building could not ignore. Therefore planning for new premises was executed. Foundation stone of new building of college was laid down by pundit Jawahar Lal Nehru,then Prime Minister of India, on 3 November 1958.
